Our Story

Japinos started off with a soft opening to see how the crowd reacted to our food and officially opened on September 29, 2019, at Festival Mall Alabang. We believe in serving fresh, tasty and afforadable Filipino favorites with a Japanese twist!






Our Team

Chef Angel

The heart of Japinos and the head of our kitchen. Having studied Culinary Arts at At-Sunrice GlobalChef Academy, Chef Angel puts her skills to the kitchen. She puts love and passion in to everything that she makes.

Chef Fonzy

Our youngest chef in the crew. He's finishing his Diploma in Culinary Arts at At-Sunrice GlobalChef Academy, completing his internship for six months at Japinos.
